Vanagoo for Business

Reliable rides for your employees. Lower cost for your business.

Our partners

Only In-Policy Trips Allowed

Uber automatically ensures that only trips that follow

your policy can be charged to your company account.

Set a Policy, Ensure Compliance

You decide when and where your employees can

ride—for example, only from the office after 9pm.

Simplify Travel Management

Add or remove employees from your account and see

trip info for every ride. All trips are billed to one

payment method, so all expenses are in one place.

Take the Hassle Out of Work Travel

With ETAs averaging less than 5 minutes, Uber takes the stress out of traveling for work. Your employees can now focus on the business and not the trip.

1Employee requests ride

2The Uber app ensures the ride follows company policy

3The trip is automatically billed to the company—no cash, no expensing, no reimbursing

Safe Rides

Our technology makes it possible to focus on safety for

riders and driver-partners before, during, and after a trip.

Background Checks

All potential Uber driver-partners are required to

undergo a screening process, which includes a driving

and criminal history check.

End-to-end Insurance

From pickup to drop off, your ride is covered by

commercial auto insurance. Learn more about our

safety standards.
